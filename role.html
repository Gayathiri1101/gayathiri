🪨 Gua Kelam Staff Role

Pembantu Khidmat Am | Role Overview

🧭 Visitor Assistance

Helps tourists explore Gua Kelam safely and guides them around the area.

🧹 Maintenance Duties

Ensures cleanliness and safety of the cave and surrounding environment.

💡 Information Support

Provides information about Gua Kelam history, rules, and attractions.

⭐ Safety Management

Ensures visitors follow safety rules and have a safe experience.

⭐ Customer Care & Safety

He plays an important role in ensuring visitors follow safety guidelines. He also helps manage visitor flow to avoid overcrowding and ensure comfort.

⭐ Environmental Protection

Helps preserve the natural beauty of Gua Kelam and promotes sustainable tourism practices.
